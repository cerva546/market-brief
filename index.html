About This Publication

What is Mkt Brief?

Mkt Brief is a daily financial digest designed to give general readers a clear, concise picture of what moved markets each morning. Each edition is generated at 8 AM Eastern Time and served identically to all readers that day.

Each briefing draws on market data and the latest financial headlines, covering equity indices, rates, commodities, major movers, and what to watch next.

How it works

At 8 AM ET, a scheduled server task generates the day’s brief once, stores it in Upstash, and every visitor then receives that same edition instantly via /api/brief.

Data sources

  • NewsAPI.org — Business headlines
  • Finnhub — Stock and ETF quotes
  • CoinGecko — Cryptocurrency prices
  • Bloomberg, Reuters, WSJ, FT, CNBC — Sourced via NewsAPI headlines
